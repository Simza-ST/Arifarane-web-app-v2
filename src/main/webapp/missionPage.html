Our Mission - Arifarane

All for you, all for us, together we stand to enhance and expand our agricultural skills to alleviate poverty in our homes and show care to our world.

Goals and Objectives

Objectives:

  • Appoint competent deputy chairperson, secretaries.
  • Establish the media, event, financial, and administration teams.
  • Proposal for maize meal donation and other supplements to form a full meal.
  • List and verify Arifarane Beneficiaries.
  • Achieve 60+ identification accuracy.
  • Documentary of full information about Arifarane (Subcommittees).
  • Engage crop science, public affairs, and journalism students.
  • Organize 12 activities for the year.
  • Spread positive media image of TUT.
  • Help a minimum of 10 students.
  • Retain the best advocacy organization.
  • Participate in local radio interviews and newspaper articles.
  • Complete 80% of planned projects.
  • Attend a minimum of 2 external events (workshops).

Long-term Goals:

  • Provide a full balanced meal for disadvantaged students.
  • Establish gardens at households.
  • Expand to every TUT campus.
  • Collaborate with external establishments.
  • Implement meal kitchens and food drives.
  • Offer agricultural support to external forces.

Short-term Goals:

  • Harvest and provide for a minimum of 10 students.
  • Structure the committee and assign essential roles.
  • Inaugurate additional reinforcement.
  • Open alliance with stakeholders.
  • Source motivation and inspiration for members.
  • Establish an operational garden maintenance system.
  • Produce seedlings for future planting.
