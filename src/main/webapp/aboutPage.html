About Arifarane

Arifarane is an organization established by Tshwane University of Technology (TUT) students for students by students. Our mission is to tackle the challenge of poverty through agriculture. We are more than just an organization; we are a family that cares about each other and those around us.

Arifarane also engages in initiatives like Go-Green and empowers the youth in agriculture to eradicate poverty in our communities. We offer essential skills such as administration, group dynamics, leadership, teamwork, and management to develop students.

Our vision is to create a sustainable agricultural project within TUT to grow and supply basic food produce for students in need, assisting them in coping with university life.

Purpose

  • Lend a helping hand to students to improve their standard of living, starting within the organization.
  • Create a practical learning environment towards the exposed world.
  • Provide a learning and entertaining environment to develop employable skills.
  • Develop a sustainable agricultural project within TUT through student-led initiatives.
  • Grow and supply basic food produce for students in need to assist them in adjusting to university life.
