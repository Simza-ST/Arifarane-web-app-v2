ARIFARANE

In support of the Green-Campus initiative Arifarane has taken the first step in going green with the garden, the organisation is extending in engagement in the initiative. 2023 Arifarane dedicated a day to clean Student-Town residence as an act of environmental health. 

Organization Purpose

The purpose of the Organization is to improve the environmental state of the campus and encourage students to take interest in the well-being of the environment.
